This view renders all MVG bikes available at the selected date and time as heatpoints on a map. The bigger the displayed heat point, the more bikes are contained in the heatpoint.

To get a clearer distinction where the bikes in a highlighted area are, you can zoom in.
When you change the data or time you have to click Refresh to render data for the new selection.
Only dates for which data is stored can be selected with the datepicker.
